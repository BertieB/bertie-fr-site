What I do

Services for NGOs and charities worldwide

Digital Awakening

After 50+ digital transformation projects, I know digital can't «transform» NGOs. But I have an alternative programme to successfully reposition digital within your organisation. This is not about technology alone – it's about mindset, processes, and people working together.

Strategies

I work with key stakeholders to ensure your organisation's strategies (fundraising, comms, campaigning…) resonate with a connected audience. This covers digital strategy, audience development, and multi-channel planning.

Healthcheck

I produce a comprehensive audit of your digital channels covering accessibility, content, marketing, and technology – with a detailed report to improve conversion. A clear, actionable document your whole team can work from.

Online Fundraising

I work alongside your team to build data-led digital fundraising campaigns that convert – with the right amount of innovation and creativity. From strategy to execution, I help you reach and engage donors effectively online.

Tech signposting

I gather your requirements and help you select the right CMS, email automation tool, donation platform, and eCRM. I scope pilot projects and test emerging technologies so you don't have to. No vendor allegiances – just honest advice.

Upskilling

I assess the digital capability of your staff to prepare and deliver a bespoke digital training programme. I mentor directors and coach senior managers. Training is tailored to your sector, your tools, and your team's starting point.

Other services

I regularly speak at conferences, facilitate workshops, and help with staff recruitment. I sometimes work with other consultants, agencies and technology partners to deliver larger programmes.

  • Conference speaking & keynotes
  • Workshop facilitation
  • Staff recruitment assistance
  • Collaboration with agencies & technology partners
